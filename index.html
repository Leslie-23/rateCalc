How to use this tool

Sending GHS

Customer gives GHS, receives NGN.
NGN = (GHS ÷ send rate) × 1000

Example: ₵200 ÷ 8.5 = 23.53 × 1000 = ₦23,530
Sending NGN

Customer gives NGN, receives GHS.
GHS = (NGN ÷ 1000) × receive rate

Example: ₦200,000 ÷ 1000 = 200 × 7.9 = ₵1,580
Receiving GHS

Customer wants GHS, pays NGN.
NGN to pay = (GHS wanted ÷ receive rate) × 1000

Example: Want ₵200 ÷ 7.9 = 25.32 × 1000 = ₦25,320
Receiving NGN

Customer wants NGN, pays GHS.
GHS to pay = (NGN wanted ÷ 1000) × send rate

Example: Want ₦200,000 ÷ 1000 = 200 × 8.5 = ₵1,700
Rate Format

Send Rate 8.5 = ₵1 = ₦8.5? No! It means ₵1 = ₦8.5 × 1000 = ₦8,500

We work in thousands of Naira for easier math.
