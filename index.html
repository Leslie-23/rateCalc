How to use this tool

Send Rate

Customer gives you GHS, you give them NGN. Set how many NGN you give per 1 GHS.

Receive Rate

Customer gives you NGN, you give them GHS. Set the NGN cost per 1 GHS.

Tip

Type in either the GHS or NGN field — the other updates automatically. Works in both directions.

Auto-save

Your rates are saved on this device and will be here when you return.
